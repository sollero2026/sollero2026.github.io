The Demoman in an insanely versatile class capable of almost everything from denying areas, being a fronline menace, to flying around hitting people with a bottle. As the demoman uses explosives, he can also rocket-jump like soldier, just in his own verion, it's called "Sticky-Jumping" if you're using your stickybomb launcher and "Grenade/Pill Jumping" if you're using your grenade launcher. His grenade launcher can be used to dish out respectable damage quickly, his stickybomb launcher may be slower, but it has the single highest burst damage potential in the entire game, and as the demoman solely uses explosive damage, he can get harmed by these weapons up close, so the bottle has that covered, being able to serve as a finisher up close without taking any damage and risking killing yourself.
