Pyro is pretty everage class in terms of health and speed, however, it is a close range menace with it's shotgun and flamethrower. In any sense, despite being listed as an [Offense] class, the pyro is actually more like a combination of all three class divisions, it can support his team by extinguishing them and pushing back enemy players via "airblast", it can defend a location via the aformentioned "airblast", the pyro's close range also makes it more defensively oriented as it can use ambushes in order to punish the enemy severly for coming too close to it. It can also deal some serious damage in the frontlines, this is achieved by both it's flames and airblast, which not only pushes back people, but also refects almost every projectile back to the person who shot it, dealing 133% of it's base damage.
