The medic is probably the most influencial and important class in all of tf2, the enemy will be more than willing to sacrifice themselves in order to end your life. Just by merely existing and healing a few players you become a massive threat to the opposing team. Medic heals people, which is very important in a game like TF2, where health is scarce. The Medic also has a sort of "ultimate move" that he must first charge up by healing people, this is called an "Ubercharge" and it will usually decide which team will win or lose. If you play as medic, you can easily turn the tides of battle and win the game using this ability. The Ubercharge has diferent effects based on the sort of medigun you're using, it can do things like make you and your patient invincible for 8 seconds or do triple damage for the same duration, it is a very powerful tool that must be used wisely.
