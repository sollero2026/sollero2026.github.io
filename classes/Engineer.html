The engineer is a very powerful class, however, his buildings require time to set up, making him perfect for setting up defenses that are very hard to pass, these are what we call "engineer nests". The Engineer has three types of buildings, sentries, dispensers, and telporters. Sentries shoot people, Dispensers give health and ammo to members of his team, and Teleporters, well, teleports people, saving important time. If you're alone and happen to stumble upon an engineer nest, don't expect to come back alive.
