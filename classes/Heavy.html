The Heavy is the slowest class of all, and without things like "rocket jumping" to move around faster, he heavily relies on his positioning, aim, and sheer tankiness His gun also needs to "rev" up before being able to shoot, amplifiying his slowness even more. However, his gun also has the single greatest consistent dps in any weapon in the entire game, being able to kill all classes in less than a second in point blank range (given, of course, that the gun has already been revved). Due to his seemingly lackluster skill floor, heavy is very appealing to those who just started playing the game.
