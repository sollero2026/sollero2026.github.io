Spy's job is to eliminate high-priority targets, like medics, snipers, and skilled players, not unlike Sniper. Unlike Sniper though, Spy has to infiltrate the enemy in order to do his job properly. The "Backstab" is Spy's primary way of killing enemies, one poke from his knife will instantly kill any class, which is why he needs to go near the enemy. This can be acheived using his Invis Watch and Disguise Kit. The Invis Watch allows him to sneak past the frontlines without being noticed, whilst the Diguise Kit allows him to blend among the enemies.
