The Cafe

Founded in the city of Manaoag in 1993, C-sketch, also known as Cafe sketch, has been providing impeccable service to their customers, both food-wise and entertainment-wise.
Made famous because of their irreplicable customer service, including but not limted to; art boards, a mini book store, and hand made interior furniture, C-Sketch has become one of the most respected and revered cafes in the country
